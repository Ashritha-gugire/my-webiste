About Me

I am a dynamic Data Analytics specialist currently serving as a Research Data Analyst at George Mason University, Center for Assurance Research and Engineering, with Professors Jean Pierre Auffret and Abhishek Ray, collaborating with Deloitte. My work centers on applying advanced analytical techniques to urban mobility, knowledge graphs, and agent movement pattern analysis. I specialize in leveraging AI/ML methods to derive insights that inform decision-making, particularly in urban planning and behavioral analytics.

With an M.Sc. in Data Analytics Engineering from George Mason University, my expertise spans from data analysis methodologies, including Natural Language Processing, Business Intelligence, and anomaly detection. I actively contribute to research initiatives that bridge complex data structures with actionable insights. My approach integrates both inductive and deductive reasoning to simplify multifaceted data challenges, driving impactful outcomes for organizations and research groups.
